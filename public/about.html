About Integrated Accounts

We help small businesses stay on top of the numbers with tidy ledgers, clear reporting and proactive support.

Our story

Integrated Accounts was founded to make reliable, modern accounting accessible to busy owners. We combine practical experience with cloud tools so you get accurate books, on time, without the hassle.

From day one our focus has been: clear communication, tidy records, and timely submissions — the basics done properly, every month.

Our approach

  • Cloud-first: Xero and connected apps for smooth, secure workflows.
  • Consistency: Standardised processes, reconciliations and schedules.
  • Proactive: We flag issues early and keep you ahead of deadlines.
  • Plain English: Numbers translated into useful actions and next steps.
