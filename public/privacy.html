Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information.

Last updated: 10 September 2025

Who we are

Integrated Accounts Ltd (“we”, “us”) provides bookkeeping, accounts and related services in the UK.

Contact: info@integratedaccounts.co.uk · +44 01273 053556

The data we collect

  • Contact data — name, email, phone, company.
  • Business data — accounting records you share with us when you become a client.
  • Usage data — basic analytics (pages visited, device type). We do not profile individuals.
  • Communications — emails and messages you send to us.

How we use your data (lawful bases)

  • Provide services you request (contract).
  • Respond to enquiries (legitimate interests).
  • Comply with law and professional obligations (legal obligation).
  • Improve our site and services (legitimate interests, anonymised where possible).

Sharing

We only share data with trusted providers as needed to deliver our services (e.g. email/hosting, accounting software) and where appropriate data processing terms are in place. We never sell your data.

Retention

Enquiry data is kept for up to 12 months. Client records are retained in line with UK accounting/tax requirements (typically 6 years), then securely deleted.

Security

We use encrypted transport (HTTPS), access controls and least-privilege practices. If we become aware of a data breach that affects you, we will notify you and the ICO where required.

Your rights (UK GDPR)

  • Access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection.
  • To exercise rights, email info@integratedaccounts.co.uk.
  • You can complain to the UK ICO: ico.org.uk.

Cookies & analytics

We use minimal, privacy-respecting analytics to understand site usage. You can block cookies in your browser; the site will still work.

International transfers

Where providers are outside the UK/EEA, we rely on adequacy regulations or standard contractual clauses to protect your data.

Third-party links

Our site may link to other websites we do not control. Their privacy practices are their own.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The revision date appears at the top of this page.
